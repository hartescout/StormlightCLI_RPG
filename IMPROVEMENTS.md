# Stormlight RPG - Improvements Summary

## New Content

### 3 New Radiant Orders
- **Skybreaker**: Justice-focused with Gravity + Division surges
- **Edgedancer**: Support/evasion with Friction Slide + Regrowth
- **Elsecaller**: Versatile scholar with Soulcast + Teleportation

### 7 Enemy Types (up from 2)
- Parshendi Warrior (basic)
- Parshendi Archer (ranged)
- **NEW:** Parshendi Sharpshooter (multi-shot ability)
- **NEW:** Parshendi Berserker (rage mode)
- **NEW:** Parshendi Shardbearer (armor-piercing)
- **NEW:** Midnight Essence (stormlight drain)
- **NEW:** Chasmfiend (boss-level threat)

### 7 Bridge Run Events (up from 3)
- Arrow Volley
- Chasm Jump
- Wounded Bridgeman
- **NEW:** Falling Boulder
- **NEW:** Chasm Treasure
- **NEW:** Parshendi Drummer
- **NEW:** Bridge Collapse

## Gameplay Changes

### Equipment System
- **Weapons**: 11 different weapons with damage bonuses (+1 to +15)
- **Armor**: 4 armor types with defense ratings (2-10)
- **Shop**: Purchase weapons and armor at the Apothecary
- **Inventory**: Manage and swap equipment from your inventory

### Combat Balance
- Enemy HP increased (30-200 HP range)
- Surge costs rebalanced (6-30 Stormlight)
- Surge damage reduced to balanced ranges
- Armor now reduces incoming damage
- Enemies have 30% chance to use special abilities

### Enemy Special Abilities
- **Piercing Shot**: Ignores 50% of armor
- **Multi-Shot**: Multiple weaker attacks
- **Rage**: Massive damage burst
- **Shard Strike**: Ignores all armor
- **Drain Light**: Steals Stormlight + damage
- **Claw Swipe**: Heavy boss attack

### Quality of Life
- **Ideal Naming**: Properly displays "1st, 2nd, 3rd" instead of always "2nd"
- **Balanced Leveling**: Partial heal (50%) on level up instead of full
- **Flee System**: 60% success rate, loses spheres, failed flee = free attack
- **Better Errors**: Clearer error messages for missing/corrupt files
- **Safe Prompts**: Removed default values that could cause accidental actions

## Strategy Tips

- **Equipment matters**: Invest in armor early for survivability
- **Special abilities**: Enemies are more dangerous - dodge buffs are valuable
- **Resource management**: Don't spam expensive surges
- **Flee wisely**: Sometimes living to fight another day is worth a few spheres
- **Edgedancer healing**: Regrowth is powerful for sustained combat
- **Boss fights**: Save high-cost surges for Shardbearer/Chasmfiend encounters
